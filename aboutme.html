About the Conductor

Star is a super-unripe vegetarian from Harlem. She gave up all critters and creatures that walk, crawl, slither, swim, etc., after one too many animal documentaries paired with wine. Star enjoys going out to eat with friends and initially one of the challenges she faced was finding vegetarian-friendly meals in restaurants. Star is now on a mission to share every vegetarian and vegan-friendly meals she finds throughout the boroughs of Manhattan, the Bronx, and Brooklyn. She hopes to inspire everyone around to try a vegetarian meal while skipping town.

Thanks for riding the V Train and have a safe and fun trip!
