"This is a VEGETABLE bound V-Train, stand clear of the closing doors please!"

The V-train is an express ride through NYC, taking passengers to some of the best vegan and vegetarian restaurants throughout New York City. The V train- much like the 2,4 and 5 trains- starts in the The Bronx and goes through Manhattan, making it's final destination in Brooklyn. The V also allows for connection to the Shuttlebus for connection to other destinations as honorable mentions.
