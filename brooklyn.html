Brooklyn Stop:
  • M.O.B.!

    • M.O.B. is a completely VEGAN restaurant located near the Atlantic Terminal in Downtown Brooklyn! I chose this spot to present because it's one of my favorites in Brooklyn and it's one where you won't have to wait forever to sit and enjoy a great meal. M.O.B. is a communal style eatery with rather interesting decor. Pictured above I indulged in a delicious "MOB dog" made from chickpeas, spiced carrot and mushrooms and garnished with sauerkraut, ketchup and mustard. One word.... HEAVENLY. What I love about M.O.B. is everything is freshly made and full of rich flavors. For those who may already be vegan and used to preparing meals at home, this is a great go-to stop. :)

    Bon appetit!
