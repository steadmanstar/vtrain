Bronx Stop:
  • Jahlookova Organic Vegetarian Restaurant

    • Jahlookova is a vegetarian restaurant located right off the Concourse (B/D line) on 170th street and disclaimer: it is NOT a sit-in style restaurant. It offers however, a variety of healthy/health-conscious food & drink for anyone on the go. Jahlookova has a variety of fresh juices and smoothies, salads and hearty vegetarian meals with a Caribbean/West-Indian flare. If you're from the BX you'll feel right at home in this sweet spot. What I love about Jahlookova is that it's absolutely a rare gem in that it provides organic foods to the community. Good vibes only!

    Buen provecho!
