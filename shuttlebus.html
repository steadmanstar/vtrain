The Shuttlebus!

The SHUTTLEBUS is a means of transit to get you from select NYPL branches to nearby vegetarian and vegan hotspots. Here are some of these hotspots. :)


Directly across the street from The Bronx Library Center is

  • 301 Cafe
    • which serves a variety of veggie friendly foods including fresh juices, salads and hummus.


    Two blocks south of the Countee Cullen Branch in Harlem is

  • Rejuvenate Juice Bar
    • which like 301 Cafe also serves fresh juices, smoothies, soup and Trini patties!


    Now this one is a bit of a stretch... but if you're ever at The Brooklyn Public Library either at the Central Library branch, the Bedford branch or hopefully somewhere in between you have to stop by

  • Vegetarian Palate
    • . Located in Prospect Heights this a staple restaurant in the community serving completely meat-free and vegetarian spins of classic Chinese-style cuisine. Bonus: They Deliver!
