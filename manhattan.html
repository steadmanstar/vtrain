Manhattan Stop:
  • Penelope NYC!

    • Penelope is a cute, chic and comfortable restaurant in Kips Bay that caters to both herbivores and carnivores. During my recent visit I feasted on a veggie burger (shown above) while my friend dined on their hearty spinach pie accompanied by a zesty salad. I'm a burger fanatic and this is absolutely the best one I've ever eaten. The texture, consistency, as well as savory flavors makes this an easy favorite of mine and also a great choice for anyone who has never tried a veggie-based burger. Be sure to try their polenta fries... and for fish eaters, I've also heard the salmon burger is pretty yummy. Penelope is also a neighborhood favorite for their breakfast menu and is always packed in the AM!

    Enjoy!
